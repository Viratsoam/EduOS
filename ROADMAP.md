# EduOS Roadmap

## Phase 1: Core Platform

Goal: build the minimum complete Education OS.

Scope:

- Authentication
- Organizations
- RBAC
- Users
- Courses
- Subjects
- Lessons
- Students
- Teachers
- Teacher dashboard
- Student dashboard
- Live classes
- Chat
- AI Tutor
- Assignments
- Exams
- Analytics

Success criteria:

- An organization can onboard.
- Admins can manage users and permissions.
- Teachers can create courses and lessons.
- Students can learn, ask the AI Tutor, submit assignments, and take exams.
- AI answers are grounded in approved course content.

## Phase 2: Monetization and Ecosystem

Scope:

- Payments
- Marketplace
- Subscriptions
- Certificates
- Parent portal
- Calendar integrations
- Meeting integrations
- Drive integrations

Success criteria:

- Organizations can manage plans and billing.
- Students can receive certificates.
- Parents can view learner progress.
- Live class and calendar workflows are integrated.

## Phase 3: Advanced Learning

Scope:

- Mobile apps
- Offline learning
- AI agents
- Voice tutor
- Whiteboard
- Proctoring
- Advanced weakness detection

Success criteria:

- Learners can study across devices.
- AI can proactively support learning plans.
- Live and exam workflows support advanced classroom use cases.

## Phase 4: Global Education Network

Scope:

- Skill marketplace
- Hiring portal
- Research platform
- Global content marketplace
- University network
- Enterprise learning

Success criteria:

- EduOS becomes a network for institutions, educators, learners, employers, and content creators.
