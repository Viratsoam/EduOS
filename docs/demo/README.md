# EduOS Product Vision Film

This folder contains the reusable, AI-assisted product vision film for EduOS.

Once GitHub Pages is enabled, open [the hosted EduOS Product Vision Film](https://viratsoam.github.io/EduOS/) in a modern browser and choose **Export WebM video**. It renders a 62-second, 1280 x 720 captioned video locally; no account or upload is required.

The film presents the planned end-state product rather than claiming every feature is already available. It covers:

1. An institution-wide operating view.
2. Organization onboarding and tenant setup.
3. Role-aware workspaces for people across the institution.
4. Connected courses, batches, lessons, resources, assignments, and exams.
5. An AI Tutor restricted to approved learning material with citations and refusal behavior.
6. Live classroom operations and follow-up.
7. Learning analytics that lead to timely intervention.
8. The platform vision: one operating layer that grows from a tutor to a university network.

`eduos-vision-cover.png` is an AI-generated visual created for the opening and closing of the film. The renderer uses it alongside illustrated product surfaces so the video communicates the full product direction clearly while the live app continues to evolve.
